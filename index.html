  3. Opening and closing body tags
  4. Inside the body tags the content of the webiste is written.

    1. The Headings tags,These are used to determine the size of the headings.It includes:
      • h1
      • h2
      • h3
      • h4
      • h5
      • h6 and more....
    2. The p tags,which has is used to write all the paragraph content of the website.
    3. Thebr tag,used give a line gap.
    4. Thehr tag,this is used to bring a horizontal line.
    5. Theem tags,this tags are used to change the words or a sentence into italic form( italic form ).
    6. Theimg src="" used to insert a image.
